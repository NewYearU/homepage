Jie Wang, MD, PhD

Critical Care Medicine | Echocardiography | Artificial Intelligence in Medicine

I am a physician-scientist in critical care medicine with research interests in echocardiographic phenotyping, hemodynamic monitoring, and artificial intelligence applications in critical illness.

About

My work focuses on bridging bedside physiology and computational approaches to better understand circulatory dynamics and cardiac dysfunction in critically ill patients. I am particularly interested in AI-assisted echocardiography, sepsis-induced cardiac dysfunction, and clinically meaningful hemodynamic trajectories.

Research Interests

  • AI-driven echocardiographic analysis
  • Hemodynamic monitoring and circulatory coherence
  • Sepsis-induced cardiac dysfunction
  • Macro–micro circulatory dynamics in critical illness

Selected Publications

  • Hemodynamic homeostasis disequilibrium in critical illness. Frontiers in Physiology, 2025.
  • Time course of morbidity and mortality across echocardiographic phenotypes in patients with sepsis: a systematic review and meta-analysis. Critical Care Medicine, in press.
